Organization Recommendation

Sustainable Philippines
"Sustainable Philippines" provides a comprehensive resource on sustainable living practices tailored to the Filipino context. The blog covers topics such as urban gardening, sustainable agriculture, plastic waste reduction, and eco-friendly innovations in the Philippines. It features guest contributions from students, experts, and environmental advocates sharing their knowledge and experiences..
Gawad Bayani ng Kalikasan
This blog highlights Filipino environmental heroes and changemakers who have made significant contributions to environmental conservation and sustainability. It features interviews, profiles, and success stories of students and individuals leading environmental initiatives and making a positive difference in the Philippines.
Eco Warrior PH

"Eco Warrior PH" is a dynamic and inspiring online platform dedicated to promoting environmental awareness, sustainability, and eco-conscious living in the Philippines. As a trusted source of green insights and eco-friendly solutions, Eco Warrior PH empowers Filipinos to become environmental stewards and advocates for positive change.
Green Convergence
"Green Convergence" is a leading environmental alliance and movement in the Philippines, uniting diverse groups, organizations, and individuals who share a common commitment to safeguarding the environment and promoting sustainable development. As a powerful force for positive change, Green Convergence advocates for holistic and inclusive approaches to environmental conservation and addresses pressing environmental challenges faced by the nation.
Philippine Native Plants Conservation Society
This organization focuses on promoting the conservation of native Philippine plant species and their habitats. Their website provides information on native plants, conservation efforts, and educational resources to raise awareness about the importance of protecting indigenous flora.
Save Philippine Seas
Save Philippine Seas is an organization dedicated to marine conservation and protecting the country's marine resources. Their website features educational content, citizen science initiatives, and ways for individuals to support marine conservation efforts.
Haribon Foundation for the Conservation of Natural Resources
The Haribon Foundation is one of the leading environmental organizations in the Philippines, dedicated to the conservation of the country's natural resources. Their website provides valuable information on various environmental campaigns, biodiversity conservation, and ways for individuals to get involved and support their initiatives.
